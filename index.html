Credit Transfer

Degree Credit Transfer


As a student of any recognized university or college, you have the freedom to apply for a transfer of credits from your current university to a new University. NES Institute of Distance Education help students to successfully transfer their credits from their existing university to a new university. Students can approach us for credit transfer details with their existing mark lists.

What is Degree Credit Transfer?


Degree credit transfer allows students to transfer course from their existing university to a new UGC approved university. Degree Credit Transfer University Program is a legal procedure in India which is approved by the UGC and Central government. By using credit transfer program, students will not lose any of their existing credits.

Benefits of University Credit Transfer


A credit transfer system provides students with an opportunity to continue their studies even if they had to discontinue it or if they want to clear out back paper or arrears. University credit transfer is the best option to keep the education going even when you have faced certain blocks. Students are able to transfer colleges through legal methods laid down by the UGC.
